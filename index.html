Sreelekha Guggilam

I am a Research Associate in Machine Learning at the Geospatial Sciences and Human Security Division at Oak Ridge National Lab. I completed my PhD in Computational Sciences from Institute of Computational Data Sciences (ICDS) at the University at BUffalo (SUNY) under the guidence of Dr. Abani Patra and Dr. Varun Chandola. My research is in the area of scalable anomaly detection and clustering for temporally evolving, high-dimensional data, and time series databases.
